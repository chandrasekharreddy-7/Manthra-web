About Aarogya Hospital

Fifteen years in Banjara Hills, grown one department at a time.

Our story

Aarogya opened in 2011 as a 40-bed nursing home on Road No. 3, started by Dr. Suresh Babu after ten years in general medicine practice elsewhere in Hyderabad. Pediatrics was added in 2013 once it was clear how many young families were in the neighbourhood, followed by cardiology in 2015 and orthopedics in 2017 as referrals grew.

The remaining departments — neurology, gynecology, dermatology, ENT, dental, and ophthalmology — were built out between 2018 and 2023. We're still a single-site hospital by choice: it's easier to keep quality consistent across 120 beds than across several smaller branches.

We take walk-ins for most departments and keep same-day slots open for anyone who calls before 11 AM. If you need to be seen and can't wait for a scheduled appointment, tell the front desk — that's usually enough to get you in sooner.
