About Aarogya

A neighbourhood hospital, built up department by department

Aarogya opened in 2011 as a 40-bed nursing home in Banjara Hills. Word-of-mouth referrals from our general medicine and pediatric patients funded the cardiology and orthopedic wings that followed, and we've kept adding departments as the neighbourhood has grown.

Health Tips

From our general medicine team

Don't skip your annual blood work

Most early-stage diabetes and thyroid cases we see are caught on a routine panel, not because of symptoms. If it's been over a year, book one.

Blood pressure creeps up quietly

It rarely causes symptoms until it's already a problem. A five-minute check at any pharmacy is enough to know where you stand.

Finish the full course of antibiotics

Stopping early because you feel better is one of the most common reasons infections come back harder, and resistant to treatment.
