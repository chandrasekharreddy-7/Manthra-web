Departments

What we treat

Cardiology

Chest pain, hypertension, arrhythmia, angiography, and post-heart-attack follow-up care.

Neurology

Migraine, epilepsy, stroke rehabilitation, nerve disorders, and Parkinson's management.

Orthopedics

Fractures, joint replacement, spine care, sports injuries, and arthritis treatment.

Pediatrics

Newborn checks, vaccination schedules, growth monitoring, and childhood illness care.

Gynecology & Obstetrics

Pregnancy care and delivery, PCOS, infertility consultation, and menopause management.

Dermatology

Acne, eczema, psoriasis, hair loss, and minor skin procedures.

ENT

Sinusitis, hearing loss, tonsillitis, ear infections, and sleep apnea evaluation.

Dental Care

Cleanings, root canals, extractions, braces, and dentures.

Ophthalmology

Cataract surgery, glaucoma screening, vision testing, and diabetic retinopathy care.

General Medicine

Fever, diabetes, thyroid disorders, routine checkups, and specialist referrals.
